ABOUT THE CLUB

Built on Passion, Discipline & a Dream

Al Teneen Al Ahmar Martial Arts Club in Al Dhaid is the realization of a lifelong dream — founded by Coach Musthafa to provide professional martial arts and fitness training in a safe, motivating, family-friendly environment.

We believe martial arts is more than technique — it builds confidence, respect, perseverance, and a strong mind and body. From first-time beginners to advanced athletes, every student trains with professional coaching and personal attention.

  • 🥋Expert training in Karate, Kickboxing, Muay Thai, Yoga, Self-Defense & Fitness
  • 🛡️Safe, family-friendly environment for all ages and levels

Train with confidence. Grow with discipline. Achieve excellence.

OUR PROGRAMS

A Path for Every Warrior

Karate

Kids • Teens • Adults

Authentic Shotokan karate — kihon, kata and kumite with belt gradings, approved by the UAE Karate Federation.

Kickboxing

Teens & Adults

High-energy striking, pad work and conditioning — build power, speed and real self-defense skills.

Muay Thai

Teens & Adults

The art of eight limbs — punches, kicks, elbows and knees with authentic pad work and clinch training.

Yoga

All levels

Flexibility, breathing and mindfulness sessions that balance hard training and calm the mind.

Self-Defense

All ages

Practical, street-smart techniques that build awareness, sharp reflexes and the confidence to stay safe.

Fitness Training

Strength & conditioning

Functional strength, agility and endurance training — stay in fighting shape all year round.
